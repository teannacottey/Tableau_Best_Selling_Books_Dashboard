# Tableau_Best_Selling_Books_Dashboard

## Context

Best-Selling Books Dataset

**Stakeholder**: Penguin Random House (the largest book publisher in the UK)
**Question**: How can we adapt our acquisition and marketing strategy to boost our commercial performance?
**Scope**:
  - Date - 2023-2025
  - Purpose: Provide Penguin Random House with insights that identify characteristics          associated with strong commercial performance and reader demand, supporting                 acquisition and marketing strategy.
  - KPIs & Insights - Author, Genre & Pricing

    
## Dataset Source Information

**Description**: “ This dataset contains three yearly snapshots of best-selling books from 2023, 2024, and 2025. It can be used to study trends in book popultarity, author recurrence, category dominance, and rank stability across years. The data was manually collected from publicly available Amazon Best Seller rankings and is intended for educational and analytical purposes . This dataset is not affiliated with or endorsed by Amazon.”   

**Source**: Malak Lahyani, Kaggle  

**Link**: https://www.kaggle.com/datasets/malaklahyani/best-selling-books-20232025?select=best+sellin+books+total.csv
